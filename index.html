The AgriFund Web Platform proves an online marketplace connecting small farmers and investors

Grow your farming business with AgriFund

This platform streamlines the process of securing funding for agricultural projects, enabling farmers to apply for loans and investors to find lucrative investment opportunities

Features

Project Listings

View all agribusiness projects listed on the platform.

This where the investor browses through a list of projects and selects the one they would like to invest in.

Create agribusiness project

Fill form to create new projects

Farmers are able to create new farming projects that they wish to secure funding through investor interests

Express Interest

Investor fills form to express interest in a project

This is where the investor looks at the project details and decides to express interest in the project. The details are then sent to the farmer to enhance offline communication.

About

INSPIRATION BEHIND THE PLATFORM

Growth of the idea

AgriFund was inspired by a need to connect small farmers to investors. Many farmers may lack the necessary funding to expand their farming activities. I was talking to a colleague friend who is an entrepreneur and peer at Holberton school, and he mentioned that he had a agribusiness project that he wanted to start but lacked the necessary funds. This is when I thought of creating a platform that would connect farmers to investors. The farmers would be able to post projects which would land in the hands of investors who are willing to Fund. This would be a win-win situation for both parties as investors find low-risk investment opportunities in agribusiness while farmers get the funding to expand their farming activities.

The platform is still in its early stages and I am looking forward to adding more features to it. I am also looking forward to collaborating with other developers to make the platform a success.
The project was also a fullfillment of Portfolio project requirement at Holberton School.
